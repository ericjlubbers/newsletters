ANOTHER AURORA SCHOOL JOINS NATIONAL MENTOR READING PROGRAM

Anya Semenoff, YourHub

Reading Partners, a national nonprofit that finds and trains volunteer tutors and matches them with students who are reading one to 2½ years below grade level, has partnered with a third Aurora elementary school.

HENDERSON NONPROFIT TURNS SHELTER DOGS INTO SERVICE DOGS FOR THE DEAF

Anya Semenoff, YourHub

One of the first organizations of its kind, International Hearing Dogs has been training dogs in Colorado to listen for alarms and other sounds for people who are deaf since 1976.

LOUVIERS COUNTRY CLUB REMAINS HEART OF COMMUNITY AFTER MORE THAN A CENTURY

In the town of Louviers — a census-designated place with 269 estimated residents tucked inside Douglas County — a building boasting a library, bowling alley and events area has been the heart of the community for more than 100 years.

BLOTTER: 30,000 BOTTLES OF BEER ON THE WALL

A woman released from detox Dec. 28 was found three hours later sitting in the snow outside the King Soopers at 8055 Sheridan Blvd. in Arvada. Police noticed a strong odor of alcohol, the woman was slurring her words, could barely stand and when asked how much she had to drink, said “30,000 beers.” She was arrested for an open whiskey bottle and transported to an emergency room. Business Q&A: Crossfire Hats, Brighton

Anya Semenoff, YourHub

"Everything I do is custom. I do one hat at a time, I do it right for that customer, and then I move on to the next one. An old friend of mine once told me that my hats are different because other hatters put their particular style into every hat, whereas my hats are not me — they're my customer. I really pay attention to that customer so that I can design a hat that's perfect for them and not for me." - Lee Keltner, hatter

Business Q&A: Powder7, Golden

Anya Semenoff, YourHub

"At first, we sold demo skis almost exclusively, then we built up to selling past-season closeouts, and eventually to selling new skis as well as clothing and accessories. But even today, selling demo skis remains a core part of our business. We set ourselves apart with our large selection of demo skis (we have over 1,000 pairs in stock). We also carry many smaller brands and harder-to-find products. Everyone on our team not only skis and knows gear really well, but they all come from strong backgrounds and are very good at what they do." - Amy Dannwolf, owner
